About RMTR eSports

INTRODUCTION

RMTR eSports is a mobile esports organization, held and manage mobile competetive teams. It is sicerely active since March 2021 year in the mobile
esports scene as underdog organization, participates in various servers T1 and T2 scrims combined, server like Imb/Apl/Nox/Rl/Exd/Gmrz/Hyper/Lm etc.

HELD TEAMS

RMTR eSports held the underdog T1 team RM, they participate in all T1/T2 or say Pro/Veteran/Elites scrims organised by all the server mentioned above.
Another held underdog team is Semi-T1 team IM, this team is as backup on hold due to all the players are completing their graducation/schooling etc.

FUTURE VISION

RMTR eSports is established having next level vision in mind to dominate in the future of mobile esports scenario, either as organiser/participants.
Being a organiser we are set to throw many well managed big prize pool tournaments only for underdogs in upcoming future and to provide them a better
and feasibly manager/organised plateform to practice on daily basis to grind in next level competetion. And as a participants, we are set to make RM/IM
players to grind at very next level that they can compete against top teams in the region in upcoming future.
